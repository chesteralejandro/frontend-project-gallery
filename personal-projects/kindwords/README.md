# KindWords

![Stack](https://img.shields.io/badge/stack-frontend-purple)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-active-brightgreen)

KindWords is a lightweight, browser-based placeholder text generator that replaces traditional lorem ipsum with something more human — **positive, encouraging, and uplifting words**.

Instead of filling your UI with meaningless Latin text, KindWords generates content that feels warm, friendly, and alive — making your development and design process a little more enjoyable.

<br />

## 🌿 Why KindWords?

Traditional placeholder text like lorem ipsum exists to mimic readable content without distraction — but it’s emotionally empty.

KindWords takes a different approach:

- 💛 Adds positivity to your workflow
- 🧠 Keeps content readable and meaningful
- 🎨 Enhances the emotional feel of your UI even in early stages

Because even placeholder text can shape how a product feels.

## 🚀 Features

- Generate uplifting placeholder text:
    - Encouraging phrases
    - Affirmations
    - Compliments
    - Motivational lines
    - And more
- Choose specific categories or generate from all
- Adjustable number of paragraphs
- Clean, minimal UI
- One-click copy to clipboard
- Fully client-side (no backend required)
- Works offline

## 🧠 How It Works

- Text content is stored in data/kindwords.json
- The generator:
    - Mixes positive words with selected category content
    - Builds paragraphs dynamically
    - Ensures a minimum character length for realistic placeholders
- Output is rendered as paragraphs and can be copied as plain text

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

**KindWords** uses a soft, warm color palette (amber/orange tones) and clean typography to create a calm, welcoming interface.

The goal is simple:

> Make development feel a little more human.
